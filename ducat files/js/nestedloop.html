Nested Loops

Q-1. Input any number from user and 
(a)display its factors
eg.  12----1,2,3,4,6,12
(b) check it is prime or not
(c) Display it's reverse number. 673---376
(d) check it is palindrome or not    eg. 121
(e)Armstrong 3 digit eg. 153   =1*1*1+5*5*5+3*3*3
(f)Perfect Number- eg. 6   sum of factors are same eg. 6=1+2+3
(g)Sum of digits eg.   435=4+3+5=12
h)Display factorial of that number.   eg.  5!=5*4*3*2*1=120
